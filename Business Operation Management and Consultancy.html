Customer Success Management

Operations consulting, also referred to as operations management, is defined as advisory and/or implementation services that improve a company's internal operations and performance in the value chain. Operations is another leafy offshoot of management consulting and bears strong similarities to strategy. The key difference is the intensive focus operations brings to a client's performance at specific points on the value chain.
